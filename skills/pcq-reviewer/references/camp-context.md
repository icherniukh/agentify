## Camp Identity

**Camp Name:** The Club
**Year:** First-year camp, BRC 2026
**Theme/Axis:** Axis Mundi (2026 event theme)
**Concept:** Participatory, gifting-based club-suit card room — casino rituals transformed into decommodified social play. No real money, no real gambling. Curios (small items, jokes, stories) replace chips.

**Core archetype:** The Joker (trickster, not clown) — bends rules, confuses expectations, transforms seriousness into absurd social performance.

## Games & Interactivity (Current State of Docs)

- **21 My Ass (Blackjack):** Guests play with a Curio (trinket, joke, story). Win = receive house Curio. Lose = their Curio goes to the House Vault (available to others). Absurd props: oversized cards, tiny dice.
- **Deez Roulette:** No Curio required to play. Win = house Curio. Lose = consent-first Joker Task from numbered task menu. Re-roll always available.
- **Joker Task Menu:** 11 numbered tasks (sample: tell a joke 10 sec; victory dance 15 sec; invent casino nickname; become "Hype Dealer" for 10 min; etc.). All opt-in, re-roll or alternative always offered.
- **House Vault Curios:** Small playa-friendly gifts — sealed non-alcoholic drinks, sun/dust utility items, trinkets.
- **Bar:** Alcohol service is present in current docs, with "No ID, No Service" policy and trained bar staff. *This is a compliance flag — see Known Gaps.*

**Stations:** 1 blackjack + 1 roulette. Second blackjack table opens at peak.

**Greeter:** Welcomes visitors, distributes initial Curios, delivers 30-second orientation + consent framing. Re-roll explained at entry.
